Privacy Policy

Plain language. No small print. Here is exactly what we collect, why, and what you can do about it.

Who is responsible for your data

Data Controller: Sharon Dehmel — Leadership Architect & Intercultural Coach

Contact: sharon@sharondehmel.com

Website: sharondehmel.com

This Privacy Policy applies to data collected through the Leadership Navigator assessment tool at leadership-navigator.vercel.app.

Last updated: April 2026

What we collect and why

When you complete the Leadership Navigator assessment, we collect:

  • Your first name — to personalise your results and playbook
  • Your company name — to understand your context and tailor recommendations
  • Your email address — to deliver your personalised First 100 Days Playbook
  • Your assessment responses — your leadership style, values, strengths, team profile, company phase, and current challenges — used to generate your personalised output

We do not collect sensitive personal data (health, financial, or identity information). We do not use cookies for tracking or advertising. The only third-party resource loaded is Google Fonts, which may log your IP address — see Google's Privacy Policy.

Legal basis for processing

We process your data on the basis of consent (GDPR Article 6(1)(a)). You give this consent by ticking the checkbox on the assessment form before starting.

You may withdraw your consent at any time by emailing sharon@sharondehmel.com. Withdrawal does not affect the lawfulness of processing before you withdrew.

How we use your data

Your data is used exclusively for:

  • Generating and delivering your personalised Leadership Navigator Playbook
  • Occasional relevant content about leadership, if you have not unsubscribed
  • Understanding patterns across respondents to improve the tool and Sharon's programs (in aggregated, non-identifiable form only)

We will never sell your data, share it with advertisers, or use it for purposes other than those listed above.

Where your data is stored

Your data is stored in Supabase, a database platform hosted on servers in the EU West (Ireland) region. This means your data never leaves the European Economic Area and is processed in full compliance with GDPR.

Email notifications are sent via FormSubmit, a third-party email relay service. Only your name, email, and assessment summary are transmitted for this purpose.

Both Supabase and FormSubmit are used as data processors under appropriate agreements and are not permitted to use your data for their own purposes.

How long we keep your data

We retain your assessment responses for 24 months from the date of submission.

After this period, your data is deleted. You can also request deletion at any time — see your rights below.

Your rights under GDPR

As a data subject in the EU, you have the following rights:

Access Request a copy of the data we hold about you.
Rectification Ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
Erasure Ask us to delete your data at any time.
Portability Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
Restriction Ask us to limit processing while a dispute is resolved.
Objection Object to processing based on legitimate interests.

To exercise any of these rights, email sharon@sharondehmel.com. We will respond within 30 days. If you are unsatisfied with our response, you have the right to lodge a complaint with your national data protection authority.

Changes to this policy

If we make material changes to how we handle your data, we will update this page and revise the "Last updated" date. We will not retroactively change the basis on which previously collected data is processed.
